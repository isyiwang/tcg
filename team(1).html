The Team

The Two Coins Group consists of the best, brightest, and most generous entrepreneurs, executives, and investors from United States, Canada, United Kingdom, and Asia.

Joshua Lee Managing Partner

Joshua Lee Managing Director

Joshua is responsible for Two Coins Group's business development, growth strategy, and overall infrastructure in the US / China. Prior to founding Two Coins Group, Joshua was responsible for leading Ernst & Young's Quantitative Services Group in the Pacific Southwest, and spent over a decade heading up their business development efforts that primarily focused on the technology and R&D sectors. His clients ranged from start-up to Fortune 100 companies, some of which included Activision-Blizzard, Znyga, Google, Bizrate.com/Shopzilla, Edmunds.com, Broadcom, and the like. Having founded several funds and projects that expand across many global markets, Joshua currently serves on several boards and is especially interested in partnering with growing companies in the mobile, gaming /entertainment, and specialized IP industries.

Derek Wong SVP Of Operations

Derek Wong Senior VP Of Operations

Derek is the co-founder of iCore Health, and has been deeply involved in the mobile and medical fitness/device industries. With a Masters degree in Mechanical engineering, Derek was the lead engineer at various technology companies, including Skyworks and Mindspeed Technologies. Most recently, he secured over $15 million sales annually with his design work and development, and continues to bring this same type of experience to Two Coins Group.

Dr. Scott Lee Director of medical affairs & applications

Dr. Scott Lee Director of Medical Affairs & Applications

Dr. Lee is adjunct assistant professor at the Stanford school of medicine, where in 2013 he won the Plager teaching award (Teacher/Professor of the year) for his teaching Stanford ophthalmology residents orbit and oculoplastic surgery. Dr. Lee also holds a faculty appointment at the School of Public Health, UC Berkeley, where he teaches 2 courses: global public health and the epidemiology of eye disease. He is active in international medical missions work, having recently taught surgeons and helped to develop sustainable surgical interventions in Macedonia, China, Myanmar, Bolivia, Morocco, Cambodia, Swaziland, Kenya, and many other countries.

 

Nate Raine Director of Marketing

Nate Raine Director of Marketing

Born in Toronto, Nate Raine’s talent for marketing was soon realized working for Vibrant Ideation & Marketing as their Marketing Specialist. With Vibrant, Nate was responsible for developing marketing strategies, as well as managing product launches for various industry-leading telecommunication companies, including Bell Mobility, HTC, and Motorola. Nate moved to Los Angeles shortly thereafter, and found himself working for a internet start-up company, as marketing manager he led a promotional campaign that reached over 29 million people, as part of the company’s sponsorship of the 2012 iHeartRadio Music Festival. Nate most recently worked with Sony and Microsoft intiating experiential marketing campaigns across Canada.

Carey Lai INTEL CAPITAl

Carey Lai

Carey Lai joined Intel Capital as an Investment Director in July 2011 and focuses on Internet-Enabled Consumer and Marketing Services, Digital Media, Technology-Enabled Cloud Services and Virally-Adopted Software. Carey joined Intel Capital from Institutional Venture Partners (IVP) where he was a Vice President focused on fast growing later-stage investments. While at IVP, his investments included ArcSight (HPQ), Business.com (DEXO), Concur (CNQR), ConnectAndSell, Danger (MSFT), Data Domain (EMC), HomeAway (AWAY), Mobile365 (SAP), SuccessFactors (SFSF), and Synchronoss (SNCR).
