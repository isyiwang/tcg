About Us

As "Venture Builders," Two Coins Group is Changing the Start-Up Model...


For many years, we have been proud to provide a variety of services for domestic and international companies in the areas of intellectual property, branding, product distribution, and capital. We have a seasoned management pool and have made a strong commitment to bring together an amazing community of entrepreneurs, investors, mentors, and technologists.

In addition to making investments in early to seed stage companies, Two Coins Group cultivates business ideas from within their own network of resources and assign internal teams to develop them (engineers, advisors, business developers, sales managers, etc.).

As venture builders, we develop many systems, models, or projects at once, and then build separate companies around the most promising ones by assigning operational resources and capital to those portfolio companies. To this end, we raise capital, staff resources, host internal coding sessions, design business models, work with our legal and accounting teams, build MVPs (minimum viable products), hire business development managers, and run very effective marketing campaigns during our ventures’ pre- and post- launch phases.
